About Heini Emilia Flowers

Heini Emilia Flowers is a floral design company that provides flower arrangement services in the Helsinki area. The business mainly focuses on wedding floristry and floral arrangement courses.

About the project

The goal of the website was to showcase my signature floral style, highlight the services offered, and make it easy for customers to get in touch. The site also serves as a portfolio, displaying past floral designs and inspiring future clients.

My tasks

I built the website using Wix, incorporating my own photographs to create a visually appealing and cohesive design. My focus was on making the site both aesthetically pleasing and easy to navigate, reflecting the beauty and elegance of floral artistry. For wedding orders, I created a questionnaire to simplify the floral arrangement planning process, making it easier for clients to communicate their preferences.
